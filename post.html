Dog Walks

Photo by Alvan Nee on Unsplash

You might be saying to yourself, "obviously it is important to take your best friend out for a walk, who wouldn't?!"

It is so beneficial for your dog in so many ways to go out for a walk, but also very beneficial for you as an owner. For one, you're walking! It helps you burn some calories and you get to explore the outdoors.

However, something so mundane as walking your dog can turn into so much more. We love walking our dog to discover something new in our neighborhood. We socialize more with these walks, with neighbors or new people at parks or hikes on trails.

Photo by Eric Ward on Unsplash


As mentioned before, health benefits are a huge factor to go out, more than just walks, they help you lower blood pressure, decrease stress, build stronger bones and muscles.

It is easy to simply get out of the house and walk a few feet so that your favorite pet simply relieve themselves and then go inside. But if you take on more longer of a walk, both of you will benefit so much more with longer walks.
